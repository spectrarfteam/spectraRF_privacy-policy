Privacy Policy for SpectraRF Effective Date: March 17, 2026 SpectraRF (“we”, “our”, or “the app”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information. 1. Information We Collect SpectraRF does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. The app only accesses device data necessary to provide its core functionality, specifically: Permissions Used Location: Used only to associate cellular signal data with geographic location for analysis and logging. Phone State (READ_PHONE_STATE): This permission allows the app to access real-time cellular network information including signal strength, network technology (LTE/5G), roaming status, and carrier details. It is required to monitor network changes and provide detailed technical analysis. 2. Data Usage All data is processed locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers. No personal data is sold or shared. 3. Third Parties SpectraRF does not use third-party analytics or tracking services. 4. User Control You can stop logging at any time from within the app. Uninstalling the app will remove all stored data from your device. 5. Updates to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available within the app and on our website. 6.
